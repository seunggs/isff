Roy Thomson Hall

Located in the heart of King Street Theatre District since 1982, this acclaimed concert hall landmark boasts a curvilinear exterior design and sloping glass canopy, which is reflective by day and transparent by night.

Roy Thomson Hall is the home of the Toronto Symphony Orchestra and the premier venue for the Toronto International Film Festival’s gala screenings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What can I use my drink tickets towards?

As a sample, the majority of your drink tickets can be redeemed for the following:

  • Tasting Flights for Spirits (1/3rd-oz shots of 3 types) = 1 ticket
  • Spirit Shots (1-oz) = 1 ticket
  • Specialty Cocktails / Drinks = 2 tickets

Please Note: some tasting flights, shots, and specialty drinks may require more drink tickets for redemption based on the premium grade and brand of the liquor. Drink ticket redemption will be clearly marked on the menus at each bar station.

How much do additional drink tickets cost?

Additional drink tickets can be purchased for $5 each (inclusive of taxes). Cash and credit card payments (MasterCard, Visa, AMEX) are accepted. Unfortunately at this time, debit cards cannot be accepted.

Will I get full from the complimentary food provided?

There will be complimentary snack foods available to all guests, while quantities last. It is recommended that all guests have a light dinner prior to attending the event, as you should never consume high levels of alcohol on an empty stomach. There is no guarantee you will have a full meal at this event from the complimentary food provided.

Can I drink as much as I want?

You must have valid drink tickets to redeem for alcoholic beverages. All bartenders at the Toronto Bar Fest are Smart Serve Certified and have the right to refuse serving alcohol to individuals who appear to be (or on the verge of being) intoxicated. This is for your own safety and the good of the community.

Will there be non-alcoholic beverages available?

Yes, we are providing all guests complimentary water.

Is there an ATM at the venue?

No, Roy Thomson Hall does NOT have an ATM on site. There are several banks nearby in the area though.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, Roy Thomson Hall is handicap and wheelchair accessible.

Will there be places to sit and lounge with my friends?

There will be chairs and tables placed on the upper mezzanine and balcony levels of Roy Thomson Hall.

How do I get to and from the venue?

Roy Thomson Hall is located a 2-minute walk away from St. Andrew Subway Station. The TTC will be operational during the hours of Toronto Bar Fest (7pm to 12am). If you require a taxi-cab, we can make arrangements for you with a taxi-cab company; however, you are responsible to pay for the full fare. If you choose to drive, please drink responsibly.
