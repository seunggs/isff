THE INDIAN POUTINE

When owner Shawn McDonald opened Disgraceland, he wanted a place where everyone could eat, vegetarian or carnivore alike. This dive bar's eclectic style and personality is best shown in this vegetarian take on our Canadian staple. An Indian-flavoured poutine featuring a vegan gravy made from grainy mustard, red wine, and curry paste. Served with cheese curds and scallions on a bed of crispy, hand-cut fries.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Click to show/hide answers

How much does the passport cost? Is there a limit?

“Passport to Poutine” costs $20 +HST per passport and can only be purchased online in advance. There is a limit of 1 passport per person.

What is included in my passport?

Each passport includes one gourmet poutine from each of the six participating poutineries, for a total of six gourmet poutines for you to try.

Do I have to visit all the poutineries in the same day? Are there date restrictions?

No, you can visit any of the six participating poutineries during their regular operating hours between November 25, 2013 to February 28, 2014 (inclusive).

Is there a limit to how many poutines I can get from each vendor? How will this be tracked?

With each passport, you can only have one poutine per vendor throughout the duration of the program. Each time you visit a poutinerie to claim your poutine, the staff will check off the corresponding poutine on your passport so you know it has been redeemed.

What are upgrade options and how do I get them?

Some of the participating poutineries have more than one kind of poutine on their menu. To upgrade to a different specialty poutine, it may cost an additional $2-$3 payable directly to the vendor in store. For details, please ask the vendors.

Are there any vegetarian options?

Yes! Disgraceland’s “The Indian Poutine” and Utopia Café & Grill’s “The Classic Poutine” are 100% vegetarian friendly. Fresco's Fish & Chips poutine can be made vegetarian by upgrading to the Mushroom Gravy. For other poutineries, please contact them ahead fo time to inquire if substituions can be made. Substitutions are made at the sole discretion of the vendors.

Why do you have suggested food and drink pairings on your website?

All of our vendors have amazing food and drink offerings that will enhance your poutine-eating experience. We’ve made it easier for you by highlighting the best of the best! Quench your thirst with a frothy beverage and make it a meal by opting for other mouth-watering dishes.

Is this a family-friendly event? What if I’m under 19 years old?

Absolutely you can! Other than the alcoholic-based pairing suggestions (must be 19+), you can enjoy these poutines at any age.

Please Note: Johnny Jackson is a late-night party scene and requires guests to be 19+ to enter the venue. However, there is a takeout window where their poutine is served, which can be accessed by those of all ages.

What if I have allergies to certain food ingredients?

Please contact the vendors ahead of time to inquire if substitutions can be made – this would be at the sole discretion of the vendors and cannot be guaranteed.
