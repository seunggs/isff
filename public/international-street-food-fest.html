International Street Food Fest 2014

Feast on gourmet street food from Toronto's top cultural kitchens.

Indulge in gourmet international street food.

It's about time we celebrate the cultural diversity our city has to offer.

Expand your palate with gourmet street food from cuisines around the world and all under one roof! International Street Food Fest is happening at Eaton Chelsea Hotel’s Market Garden outdoor patio space (near Yonge St. and Gerrard St. W., Toronto).

Enjoy cultural dishes lovingly prepared by Toronto's top eateries, alongside refreshing craft beer and live entertainment.

Event Tickets are $45 + HST per person and includes:

  • 6 cultural dishes of your choice
  • 2 full-sized craft beers (341mL each)
